Craig Belcher Memorial Scholarship

Craig Belcher and Mick Gutowski were long-time educators and coaches, whose departures, in 2011 and 2016, have left a gaping hole in the Milwaukee youth community.


To honor them, we have created a scholarship fund in their names. There is also an annual charity golf outing. Registration pages can be found on our site.
