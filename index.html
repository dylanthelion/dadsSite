16th Annual Bethesda Foundation Golf Tournament Sponsored by RBC Wealth Management

Please join us on Monday, July 25, 2016 at Oconomowoc Golf Club

Your participation and involvement will safeguard our mission of enhancing the lives of people with intellectual and developmental disabilities through services that share the good news of Jesus Christ.

The tournament features a scramble format and begins with a shotgun start. Your outing will feature championship golf on a private course, including many entertaining contests and opportunities to win prizes! A reception will follow the tournament, along with a tremendous dinner, raffle drawings and golf awards.

Foundation Information

Bethesda is a Christian organization that provides homes and other services to people with developmental disabilities to help them live the lives they want. Proceeds from this event will help ensure the Bethesda Foundation continues to provide financial security to assist the people Bethesda supports live the lives they want.
